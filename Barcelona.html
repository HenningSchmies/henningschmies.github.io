As our last station, we wanted to pass some days in Barcelona, so we booked a nice looking AirBnb and organised everything else. We flew in from Porto, and originally, the plan was that Isabel would return to Madrid and I would be flying out from Girona back to Germany.

The AirBnb was essentially the first one we booked without any real previous experience. In hindsight the choice could be considered a mistake, but it wasn't so horrible after all (if you asked me, at least :) ) The one difference was that we chose to book a shared apartment, so in essence we only had one room to ourselves, where during the previous stays we always had an entire, seperated apartment to us. The point really was that the circumstances weren't appeasing due to a lack of ...
